Sales Dashboard with Power BI

Barbas Bellfires had multiple data sources for all their sales-related information. The goal of this project was to create a single source of thruth for all sales-related information, meanwhile being accessible for all dealermanagers. Given the organization's standardization on the Office 365 platform, Power Bi was the right tool for this job. The dashboard exists out of three different reports.

  1. Sales Intake - Visualizes the ordered and planned fireplaces
  2. Budget Realization - Provides a visual representation of actual sales versus defined targets
  3. Sales Comparison - Empowers dealer managers to compare data from two variable timeframes

Take a look at the end result, but note that the dataset has been altered for demonstration purposes.
